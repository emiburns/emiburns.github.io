Semantic Differences Between Twitter-Based Expressions of Anxiety and Depression

Text-based analysis determined general semantic patterns, sentiment and LDA topics common to expressions of either anxiety or depression through the use of 10,000 anxiety and depression focused tweets pulled via the Twitter API. Ensemble learning algorithms best classified text as either anxiety or depression-focused.
